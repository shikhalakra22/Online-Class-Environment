About Online Class Environment

Online Class Environment is a place to store and share your files, lessons, and modules with your friends. Developed by Shikha Lakra and Shreya Khan under the guidance of Dr. Debasish Bera.

With the ongoing pandemic, more academic institutions are resorting to online services to accomodate their mixed-learning systems.

This website/web-app could be an alternative place for you (with your friends) to store and share your files!

Important note to users!

This website is only a small, non-production type project, and might still contain some problems.

Thank you for using Online Class Environment!
